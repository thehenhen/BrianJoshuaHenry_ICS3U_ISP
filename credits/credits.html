Henry Zhang

(earlydrill)

Meet Henry Zhang, a man who has devoted his life to mastering the art of programming using C++ and web development. Despite his proficiency in his field, he struggles to find fulfillment in his work and instead turns to the simple and meaningless distraction of playing "Cookie Clicker" in his free time. He finds himself feeling empty and unfulfilled, unable to shake the feeling that there must be more to life than this.

He spends his days hunched over a computer screen, typing away at lines of code, trying to find solace in his work but always coming up short. He finds himself going through the motions, completing projects without any sense of accomplishment or satisfaction.

In his free time, he turns to the game as a way to numb the feelings of emptiness and inadequacy. He gets lost in the monotonous clicking, trying to distract himself from the nagging feeling that something is missing.

But Henry is determined not to let this feeling define him. He begins to explore new hobbies and interests, seeking out activities that bring him joy and fulfillment. He starts to touch grass and make friends, using his skills to make a positive impact in his community. He also starts to mentor young programmers, passing on his knowledge and experience to the next generation.

Through these new endeavors, Henry discovers that true fulfillment comes from making a difference in the world and helping others. He begins to find purpose in his work and his life, and starts to let go of his obsession with the meaningless game. With time, he realizes that there is more to life than what he initially thought and start to live a more fulfilling life.
